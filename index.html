Essential Travel Info

Baggage Allowance

Cabin Baggage: 1 piece allowed, max 10kg. Dimensions strictly 55x35x25 cm. Items exceeding this must be checked in at an additional cost.

Checked Baggage: Not included in base fare. Can be pre-booked online for KES 1,050 per 23kg bag. Airport rates are significantly higher.

Check-in Times

Online Check-in: Opens 48 hours and closes 2 hours before scheduled departure.

Airport Counters: Open 2 hours before and close strictly 40 minutes before departure. Gates close 20 minutes before departure.

Identification

All passengers, including infants, must present a valid original National ID or Passport matching the ticket details to board.
