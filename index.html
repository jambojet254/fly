Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I miss the final payment deadline?

If the balance is not cleared 14 days prior to departure, the booking is automatically canceled. The initial deposit is non-refundable.

Can I use Jaza Pole Pole for promotional fares?

Yes, Jaza Pole Pole is applicable to all fares, including the 10-Year Anniversary Promo, provided the flight is at least 30 days away.

Essential Travel Info

Baggage Allowance

Cabin Baggage: 1 piece allowed, max 10kg. Dimensions strictly 55x35x25 cm. Items exceeding this must be checked in at an additional cost.

Checked Baggage: Not included in base fare. Can be pre-booked online for strictly KES 1,050 per 23kg bag. Airport rates are significantly higher.

Check-in Times

Online Check-in: Opens 48 hours and closes 2 hours before scheduled departure. Highly recommended.

Airport Counters: Open 2 hours before and close strictly 40 minutes before departure. Gates close 20 minutes before departure. Late arrivals will be denied boarding.

Identification & Documents

All passengers, including infants, must present a valid original National ID or Passport matching the ticket details to board. Digital copies are not accepted.

For regional flights (e.g., Goma), ensure you hold the necessary visas and Yellow Fever vaccination certificates prior to travel.

Special Assistance

If you require wheelchair assistance or have other specific medical needs, please contact our call center at least 48 hours before your flight to ensure proper arrangements are made.
